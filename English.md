# Important English Postfixes

1. Verb to Noun

    - -**al**: arrival
    - -**ure**: departure
    - -**ment**: agreement

1. Adjective to Noun

    - -**ness**: kindness
    - -**ity**: activity

1. Noun to Adjective

    - -**y**: cloudy
    - -**ous**: dangerous

1. Verb to Adjective

    - -**ive**: active
    - -**ent**: urgent
    - -**able**: payable

1. Adjective/Noun to Verb

    - -**ize/ise**: civilize, finalize, realize

1. Adjective to Adverb

    - -**ly**: actively, actually, carefully
